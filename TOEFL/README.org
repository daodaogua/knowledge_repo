** 托福四大板块

- [[reading.org][阅读]]
  - 3~4篇
  - 12~14question,each
  - 60~80m
- 阅读改
  - 减少共12题
    - 主要是词汇,细节题,句子简化题
  - 54~72m

- [[listening.org][听力]]
  - 4~6篇lecture,2~3conversation
  - 6,each,5,each
  - 60~90m
- 听力改
  - section1
    - 1conversation+1lecture
  - section2
    - 1Conversation+2lecture

- break
  - 10m

- [[speaking.org][口语]]
  - 6task
    - 2 independent
    - 4 integrated
  - 20m

- 口语改
  - 4task
    - 1independent(task2)
    - 4integrated(task3,4,6)

- [[writing.org][写作]]
  - 2task
    - 1 integrated
    - 1 independent
  - 20m, 30m
